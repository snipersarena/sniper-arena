## 🔧 Common Issues & Fixes

### ❗ GUI Doesn't Appear After Execute
**Cause:** Script was executed before the match fully loaded, or the executor injection was incomplete.  
**Fix:** Wait until you are fully spawned into an active Sniper Arena match. Detach your executor, re-inject, and execute once more. Ensure you're running the script as a single instance.

### ❗ Silent Aim Not Connecting Shots
**Cause:** Target is moving faster than the bullet velocity compensation allows, or Silent Aim FOV is too narrow.  
**Fix:** Open the GUI and increase the Silent Aim FOV angle. For long-range targets, also enable the bullet velocity multiplier in Gun Mods to ensure projectiles reach the adjusted target point.

### ❗ Aimbot Snaps to Teammates or Bots
**Cause:** Team filter is not enabled in the Aimbot settings.  
**Fix:** Open the Aimbot configuration panel and enable **Team Check** to restrict targeting to enemy players only.

### ❗ ESP Causes Significant FPS Drop
**Cause:** ESP renderer is drawing too many elements simultaneously on a low-end GPU or mobile device.  
**Fix:** In the ESP settings, disable bullet trajectory rendering and health bar display. Keep only player box ESP active. On mobile, also close background apps to free up GPU memory.

### ❗ TriggerBot Fires at Wrong Times
**Cause:** TriggerBot sensitivity is too high, triggering on partial hitbox overlaps.  
**Fix:** Open TriggerBot settings and reduce the FOV threshold. Enable **Wall Check** to prevent the TriggerBot from firing at players behind solid obstacles.

### ❗ God Mode Stops Working After Respawn
**Cause:** The game's respawn event detaches the health lock from the character model.  
**Fix:** Toggle God Mode off and back on after each respawn. Version 2.6.0+ includes an auto-reapply on respawn handler — update if you're on an older version.

### ❗ Script Crashes on Mobile Mid-Match
**Cause:** Memory pressure from running Aimbot, ESP, and Auto Kill simultaneously on a low-RAM Android device.  
**Fix:** Disable Auto Kill and bullet trajectory ESP while using Aimbot. Close all background applications before launching Roblox. If crashes persist, disable God Mode as well, as the health lock polling adds memory overhead.

### ❗ "loadstring is not available" Error
**Cause:** Your executor has `loadstring` disabled in its security settings.  
**Fix:** In your executor's settings, enable `loadstring` / `getfenv` permissions. This is required for the GUI loader to initialize properly.
